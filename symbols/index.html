NATIONAL SYMBOLS OF THE UNITED STATES OF LEAFLAND

The National Flag

The National Flag of Leafland is called the Forever Unity and it features the Union Jack in the background
and two olive branches in the foreground to represent unity. On top of the olive branches is the national
leaf: The Leaflandic Maple Leaf, to represent the Leafland State. All of it representing the country as a
whole and it's freedom and unity.

The National Coat of Arms

The National Coat of Arms is the Unity Shield, representing freedom and unity for the government.

The National Anthem

The National Anthem is called Dear People. The music is the Albanian Nation Anthem with Lyrics wriiten by President Joseph Tadd
